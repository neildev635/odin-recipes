Vegetarian Torilla Soup


Copied from Peas and Crayons.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 tsp avocado oil (or olive oil)
  • 1 bell pepper diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper diced
  • 2.5 cups vegetable broth
  • 15 oz canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 Tablespoon tomato paste
  • 15 oz can black beans
  • 15 oz can pinto beans
  • 1 cup corn
  • 3/4 cup dried red lentils
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. First, chop your veggies and measure out the ingredients.
  2. Next add everything but the heavy cream & your toppings. This includes bell pepper, jalape√±o, corn, lentils, black beans, pinto beans, broth, tomato sauce, tomato paste, salsa verde, and all your herbs + spices. Toss them in and set IP to high pressure for 15 minutes.
  3. Allow natural pressure release.
  4. Stir in the cream, add all your favorite toppings, and enjoy!
